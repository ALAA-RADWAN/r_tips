Labour force analysis by industry

Public Administration and Safety

Last updated on 2020-09-19.


A total of 822,855 individuals were employed in Public Administration and Safety in Feb 2020, before the onset of COVID-19 spread in Australia.

A total of 849,417 individuals were employed in the most recent quarter of May 2020, which is an increase of 3.23 percentage points since Feb 2020.
