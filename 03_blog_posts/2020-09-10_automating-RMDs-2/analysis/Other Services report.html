Labour force analysis by industry

Other Services

Last updated on 2020-09-19.


A total of 488,601 individuals were employed in Other Services in Feb 2020, before the onset of COVID-19 spread in Australia.

A total of 435,840 individuals were employed in the most recent quarter of May 2020, which is a decrease of 10.8 percentage points since Feb 2020.
