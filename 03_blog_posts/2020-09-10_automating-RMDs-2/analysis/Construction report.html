Labour force analysis by industry

Construction

Last updated on 2020-09-19.


A total of 1,182,671 individuals were employed in Construction in Feb 2020, before the onset of COVID-19 spread in Australia.

A total of 1,174,060 individuals were employed in the most recent quarter of May 2020, which is a decrease of 0.73 percentage points since Feb 2020.
