Labour force analysis by industry

Agriculture, Forestry and Fishing

Last updated on 2020-09-19.


A total of 324,499 individuals were employed in Agriculture, Forestry and Fishing in Feb 2020, before the onset of COVID-19 spread in Australia.

A total of 354,545 individuals were employed in the most recent quarter of May 2020, which is an increase of 9.26 percentage points since Feb 2020.
