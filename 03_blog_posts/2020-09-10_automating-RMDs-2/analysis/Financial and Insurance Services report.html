Labour force analysis by industry

Financial and Insurance Services

Last updated on 2020-09-19.


A total of 465,963 individuals were employed in Financial and Insurance Services in Feb 2020, before the onset of COVID-19 spread in Australia.

A total of 486,319 individuals were employed in the most recent quarter of May 2020, which is an increase of 4.37 percentage points since Feb 2020.
