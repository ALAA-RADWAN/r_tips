Labour force analysis by industry

Arts and Recreation Services

Last updated on 2020-09-19.


A total of 248,100 individuals were employed in Arts and Recreation Services in Feb 2020, before the onset of COVID-19 spread in Australia.

A total of 160,330 individuals were employed in the most recent quarter of May 2020, which is a decrease of 35.38 percentage points since Feb 2020.
