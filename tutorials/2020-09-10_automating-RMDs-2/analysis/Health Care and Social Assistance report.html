Labour force analysis by industry

Health Care and Social Assistance

Last updated on 2020-09-19.


A total of 1,791,824 individuals were employed in Health Care and Social Assistance in Feb 2020, before the onset of COVID-19 spread in Australia.

A total of 1,731,894 individuals were employed in the most recent quarter of May 2020, which is a decrease of 3.34 percentage points since Feb 2020.
